What is Sparsh ?

The name "Sparsh", meaning "touch" in Hindi, represents the core mission of the initiative—helping individuals understand the difference between safe and unsafe touch and ensuring that no one experiences harmful, unwanted contact.

Project Sparsh is a heartfelt initiative under Healing Up, created with a simple yet powerful purpose—to make sure every touch feels safe and every voice is heard. We know conversations around sexual harassment are often uncomfortable or brushed aside, especially for children and young adults. That’s exactly why we exist—to bring awareness, education, and healing in a way that’s relatable, supportive, and real.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower, protect, and support through education, awareness, and a future safe space for healing. We aim to provide access to resources and promote open discussions that lead to a safer, more inclusive society.

Our Vision

We aim to educate youth on safe touch, break the stigma around sexual harassment, and create a safe space for healing and support. Our long-term vision includes community outreach programs and partnerships that amplify awareness and drive real change.
