What is Sparsh?

Project Sparsh is a movement under Healing Up that educates and empowers children and young adults to understand, prevent, and respond to sexual harassment. Sparsh is a celebration of culture, creativity, and community.

Our Core Goals

Educate

Teaching about consent, boundaries, and awareness

Support

Helping survivors with guidance and strength

Empower

Giving confidence and power to speak up
